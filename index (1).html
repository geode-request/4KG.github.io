// Who We Are

Built On
Loyalty

4KG, also known as 400 Killas Gang, is a dominant and highly respected force within the Roblox scene. Built on loyalty, precision, and unity, 4KG stands as more than just a group — it's a movement. Every member represents discipline, skill, and the mindset of always staying one step ahead.

Known for their strong presence and coordinated playstyle, 4KG moves as a unit, handling challenges with strategy and control. The group values respect within its circle and maintains a reputation for being both organized and relentless in competitive environments.

With a growing name and a solid foundation, 4KG continues to expand, setting standards and making its mark wherever it goes.
